Spring in Morocco

The Earth laughs in flowers

The Beauty of the Moroccan Outdoors

In spring, the landscapes transform into a lush paradise. Here is a glimpse of the season's magic:

Inspiring Towns

Chefchaouen

Blue architecture surrounded by blooming green mountain peaks.

The Rose Valley

Kelaat M'Gouna, where the air smells of fresh Damascus roses in May.

Chefchaouen (The Rif)

Spring Highlight: The contrast of blue architecture against neon-green mountains. Perfect for high-altitude trekking among wild herbs.

Kelaat M'Gouna

Spring Highlight: The Rose Festival in May. Experience the harvest of millions of roses and the intoxicating scent of the valley.

Ifrane & Azrou

Spring Highlight: The awakening of the ancient Cedar forests and the filling of the Great Lakes (Dayats).

Akchour Valleys

Spring Highlight: Crystal turquoise waterfalls at their full power and lush moss-covered cliffs.
