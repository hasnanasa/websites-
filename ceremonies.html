Yennayer (Amazigh New Year)

Celebrated on January 14th, Yennayer marks the start of the agrarian calendar. It is a symbol of the deep roots of Amazigh culture in Morocco. Families prepare the traditional "Tagoula" and share a 7-vegetable couscous.
